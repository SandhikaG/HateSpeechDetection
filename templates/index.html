About HateShield

HateShield leverages advanced Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to identify and prevent hate speech. It analyzes both text and audio, promoting safer and more respectful online communication.

Our system is trained on a diverse dataset containing labeled examples of hate speech, offensive speech, and neutral speech, ensuring accurate classification. By incorporating supervised learning techniques and deep learning models, HateShield can detect harmful content in real-time.

Additionally, the platform aims to contribute to digital safety by helping social media platforms, organizations, and individuals mitigate online toxicity. With the integration of speech-to-text technology, HateShield extends its detection capabilities beyond text inputs, making it effective for multimedia content as well.
